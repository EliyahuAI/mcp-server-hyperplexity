## Important Notes

### Post-Money SAFE Mechanics
- **Post-money SAFE** = Investor ownership % is fixed and known immediately
- **Formula:** Investor % = Investment Amount ÷ Valuation Cap
- **$200K @ $6M cap** = 3.33% to investors
- **$300K @ $6M cap** = 5.00% to investors

### When Does the SAFE Convert?
SAFEs convert to equity when:
1. **Priced equity round** (Series A) - converts at discount or cap, whichever is better for investor
2. **Exit event** (acquisition) - converts to equity or pays out
3. **Dissolution** - investors get paid back before common stockholders

### Typical SAFE Terms
- **Valuation Cap:** $6M (you have this)
- **Discount:** 20% (optional - do you have this?)
- **Pro-rata rights:** Allows investors to maintain % in future rounds
- **Most Favored Nation (MFN):** Gets best terms if you issue better SAFEs later

### Future Dilution to Consider

1. **Option Pool (10-15%):**
   - Series A investors will want 10-15% option pool created
   - This dilutes founders and SAFE holders proportionally
   - If 15% pool: Founder goes from 95% → ~80.75% after $300K SAFE + pool

2. **Series A Round:**
   - Typical Series A: $3-5M at $15-30M post-money valuation
   - VCs typically want 20-30% of company
   - Further dilutes all existing holders

3. **Future Advisor Grants:**
   - You mentioned 1-2% for advisors
   - Typically vested over 2-4 years
   - Can grant now or after SAFE converts

## Recommendations

1. **Keep cap table simple** - Don't over-grant to advisors early
2. **Standard advisor equity:** 0.25-1% for active advisors, vested over 2 years
3. **Create option pool later** - Wait until Series A when you know hiring needs
4. **Use post-money SAFE** - You're already doing this, good choice
5. **Include discount (20%)** - Gives early investors upside if Series A values you higher
6. **Track everything** - Use Carta, Pulley, or AngelList for cap table management
