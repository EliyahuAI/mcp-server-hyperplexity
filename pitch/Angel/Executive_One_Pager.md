## THE PROBLEM: Drowning in Answers, Starving for Truth
88% of enterprises use AI, but the "Confidence Barrier" is stalling adoption. Current AI tools break after ~10 rows for multi-entity research. Real business decisions require synthesizing 100s of data points with confidence and citations. Manual verification costs 100x more and takes weeks.

## THE SOLUTION: Research Tables at Hyperspeed
Hyperplexity orchestrates 100s of AI agents to generate, validate, and update research tables. User defines research need → we decompose into hundreds of sub-queries → parallel calls to Perplexity/Claude/GPT-4 → 2-stage QC → Excel output with color-coded confidence scores and hover citations.

**Proven:** 278 rows × 23 columns | Beat ChatGPT (44 vs 10 rows) and Claude (44 vs 19) | 91% high confidence | 5-10x faster than $130M-funded Parallel.AI
