HyperPlexity Pitch — 35–40 Seconds

I left a great job leading a team of PhDs building AI for drug discovery and spent two years teaching researchers how to adopt generative AI—500 applications built, over a million in revenue. I’m showing a group of medical writers how Perplexity can get the abstract-submission date for one conference, and they love it.

Then they show me their real table: 114 conferences, 23 fields each, and say, “Great—have Perplexity fill this in.”
But it can’t. And I kept seeing that everywhere: AI answers a question, but it can’t hold an entire domain.

So we built HyperPlexity. It takes a prompt or a table and generates the whole domain—verified, trustable, and kept current.

One question → the whole domain. That’s HyperPlexity.
