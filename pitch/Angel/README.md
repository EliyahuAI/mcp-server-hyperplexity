## Common Objections & Responses

**"No paying customers yet"**
→ Product just made public. 7 users tested and impressed. Need to fix onboarding. This round validates conversion, not builds product. Marketing proven (5% CTR). B2B conversations active.

**"Moat isn't large"**
→ Agreed - that's why we move fast. 7-month head start + platform approach + marketing system = compound advantage. First mover in market education matters.

**"What if Parallel.ai adds verification?"**
→ Validates market. We're broader (ANY research vs. just entity discovery), have monitoring/repeatability, Excel integration. Platform lets us expand faster across multiple apps.

**"Solo founder risk"**
→ Strong advisory board. Built 2 complex systems in 7 months. Staying lean intentionally. First hires: marketing contractor, then sales support.

**"How is this different from academic tools?"**
→ They're paper-only. We research ANY entities (drugs, conferences, companies). General platform, not vertical tool.
